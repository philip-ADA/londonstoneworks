About London Stoneworks

Roger Sheridan is an internationally recognized expert in stonework and landscape design. A graduate of Merrist Wood College in Surrey, England, he gained much recognition by constructing gardens across Britain before moving to Chicago (from 1998 to 2022), offering his craft to Chicago, where he worked throughout the Midwest. For over two decades, he has been transforming outdoor spaces that align with his clients' visions, combining his British craftsmanship with the bold aesthetics of the Midwest. Roger relocated to Northern Indiana in 2022, establishing a new Granger-based hub with a focus on providing services in 2024.
