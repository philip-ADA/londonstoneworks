About London Stoneworks

Roger Essex has spent nearly three decades mastering the art of stonework and landscape design. A graduate of Merrist Wood College in Surrey, England, he gained early recognition by constructing two award-winning gardens at the world-renowned Chelsea Flower Show. After honing his craft with a premier London landscape construction firm, Essex brought his expertise across the Atlantic to Chicago, where he founded London Stoneworks. For over two decades, he has been transforming outdoor spaces throughout the Chicago area, combining Old World craftsmanship with contemporary design sensibilities. In 2024, Essex relocated to Granger to be near his wife's family, establishing a new Indiana base and a focus on providing services to NWI.

Services

What we do

From timeless stonework to custom outdoor living spaces, we bring craftsmanship, durability, and design excellence to every project. Explore our range of services to find the perfect fit for your home or landscape.

Lay the foundation for beauty and function with expert hardscaping. Our team installs patios, retaining walls, and other flatwork with precision and longevity in mind — blending craftsmanship and engineering for a flawless finish that lasts.

Transform your backyard into a true living space. From cozy fire pits to full-scale outdoor kitchens, we design and build features that bring warmth, flavor, and connection to your outdoor experience — perfect for entertaining or unwinding.

Create inviting outdoor spaces that connect seamlessly with your home. Whether it's a relaxing patio or a winding garden path, our paver and stone installations combine elegance, durability, and custom design for year-round enjoyment.

Add structure and comfort with custom seat walls and steps. Designed for both practicality and visual appeal, our installations define outdoor areas while enhancing accessibility and style through expert masonry detailing.

Get in touch

Roger Essex has over 27 years of experience in the stonework and landscaping industry, starting in the UK before moving to Chicago and now serving Northern Indiana and Southwest Michigan.

We provide comprehensive hardscape services including patios, walkways, driveways, retaining walls, outdoor kitchens, fire pits, and custom stonework features.

We use premium materials from top manufacturers including Unilock, Belgard, and natural stone suppliers. All materials are selected for durability and aesthetic appeal.

We focus exclusively on hardscape and stonework. We do not offer lawn care, tree services, or general landscaping maintenance.

A proper design ensures your project meets your needs, fits your budget, and complements your home. We offer 3D design services to help you visualize your project before construction begins.

Contact us for a consultation. We'll visit your property, discuss your vision, and create a custom design tailored to your space and preferences.

Our consultation fee ensures we can dedicate quality time to understanding your project and providing expert recommendations. This fee is applied toward your project if you proceed with us.

Our process includes an initial consultation, custom design creation, material selection, and professional installation. We guide you through each step to ensure your vision becomes reality.

Project timelines vary based on scope and season. Contact us to discuss your timeline and we'll provide an accurate estimate for when we can begin your project.

Our pricing reflects 27+ years of expertise, premium materials, and meticulous craftsmanship. We build projects that last decades, not just years, providing exceptional long-term value.

The best way to start is by reaching out through our contact form or giving us a call. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your vision and how we can bring it to life.
